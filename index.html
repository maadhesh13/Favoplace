Sendai Sun Plaza is a multi-purpose event complex in Sendai City, Japan, best known for its concert hall that hosts music performances, cultural events, and conferences.

It features a large-capacity hall with excellent acoustics, frequently used for live shows like Hatsune Miku’s Magical Mirai tour stops.

The building also includes hotel accommodations, banquet halls, and restaurants, making it convenient for travelers and event attendees.

Sendai Sun Plaza is located in Miyagino-ku, just northeast of Sendai Station in Miyagi Prefecture.

The nearest train station is Tsutsujigaoka Station on the JR Senseki Line, which is about a 2-minute walk from the venue.

This makes it easily accessible for both locals and visitors coming from Tokyo via Shinkansen (bullet train).


Sendai Sun Plaza is a well-known cultural and event facility located in Miyagino-ku, Sendai City, Japan, operated by Sendai City itself.

It was opened in 1993 and is designed to host concerts, ceremonies, conferences, and large-scale public gatherings.

The complex includes a hotel, multiple banquet rooms, a wedding chapel, and a large multi-purpose concert hall that is the centerpiece of the facility.

This concert hall is where Hatsune Miku: Magical Mirai 2025 is scheduled to be held in Sendai from August 1 to 3, 2025.

Specifically, the Miku concerts are conducted in the Sendai Sun Plaza Hall, which has a seating capacity of around 2,700 people and is equipped with advanced lighting and sound systems tailored for live music performances.

The venue's acoustics and stage design make it an ideal place for a high-tech vocaloid concert featuring 3D projection technology like Magical Mirai uses.

Sendai Sun Plaza is located close to Tsutsujigaoka Station on the JR Senseki Line, and just one stop away from the central Sendai Station, making access extremely convenient.

Fans visiting for Magical Mirai can also explore nearby attractions such as Sendai Castle, Zuihoden Mausoleum, and Jozenji-dori Avenue, known for its tree-lined elegance.

Destination/Location:


Sendai Sun Plaza is located at 4-1-1 Tsutsujigaoka, Miyagino-ku, Sendai City, Miyagi Prefecture 983-0852, Japan.

It is situated in the northeastern part of Sendai, just a short distance from the city’s central business and transit hub.

The nearest railway access is Tsutsujigaoka Station on the JR Senseki Line, which is only a 2-minute walk from the venue.

If you're coming from Sendai Station, it's just one stop away on the same line, or about a 15–20 minute walk if you prefer going by foot.

The building is directly accessible by bus and taxi, and it also offers parking facilities for visitors arriving by car.

Because of its central and easily reachable location, it is a prime spot for large events like Magical Mirai.




2.Disneyland Park


Disneyland Park in the USA is located in Anaheim, California, and is famously known as “The Happiest Place on Earth.”

It was the first theme park built by Walt Disney himself and opened to the public on July 17, 1955.

The park features eight themed “lands,” including Adventureland, Tomorrowland, Fantasyland, and Main Street, U.S.A., each offering unique attractions and experiences.

Some of its most iconic rides include Pirates of the Caribbean, Space Mountain, and the Haunted Mansion.

Disneyland is also home to Sleeping Beauty Castle, a central symbol of the park that draws millions of visitors each year.

In addition to thrilling rides, the park offers live entertainment, character meet-and-greets, and magical parades.

It remains one of the most visited and beloved amusement parks in the world, attracting families and Disney fans from all over the globe.

Desitination/Location:




Disneyland Park is located in the city of Anaheim, in Orange County, Southern California, USA.

It sits just off the Interstate 5 freeway, about 26 miles (42 kilometers) southeast of downtown Los Angeles.

The exact address is 1313 Disneyland Drive, Anaheim, CA 92802, which places it right in the heart of the Anaheim Resort District.

The area surrounding the park is filled with hotels, restaurants, and shopping centers, all designed to accommodate the millions of visitors who arrive each year.

Disneyland is easily accessible by car, public transportation, and airport shuttles from nearby Los Angeles International Airport (LAX) and John Wayne Airport (SNA).

Its prime location in sunny Southern California makes it a year-round destination for tourists from around the world.

3.Empire Polo Club(During Coachella Festival)


The Coachella Valley Music and Arts Festival is held every year at the Empire Polo Club in Indio, California.

This festival is one of the largest and most famous music events in the world, attracting thousands of fans from all over.

It takes place over two weekends in April and features a wide variety of music genres including pop, rock, hip-hop, EDM, and indie.

The Empire Polo Club transforms into a massive festival ground with multiple stages, art installations, food vendors, and camping areas.

The surrounding desert landscape adds to the unique atmosphere, making it a visually stunning and immersive experience.

Artists from around the globe perform here, and it's also known as a cultural hotspot for fashion, art, and celebrity appearances.

Each year, Coachella continues to grow in popularity, maintaining its reputation as a bucket-list event for music and art lovers.

Destination/Location:


The Coachella Valley Music and Arts Festival is located in the city of Indio, in Riverside County, Southern California.

It takes place specifically at the Empire Polo Club, a vast open-air venue surrounded by palm trees and mountain views.

Indio is part of the larger Coachella Valley, which is a desert region known for its warm climate and scenic landscapes.

The location is about 130 miles east of Los Angeles and is easily accessible by car or shuttle from nearby cities.

Its setting in the California desert provides a sunny and spacious environment perfect for large-scale outdoor events.

The Empire Polo Club itself spans hundreds of acres, offering plenty of room for stages, art exhibits, food courts, and camping areas.

This peaceful desert setting transforms into a vibrant and energetic festival ground every April during Coachella.
